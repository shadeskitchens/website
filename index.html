About Us

The founders of Shades Kitchens have been a beacon of luxury and sophistication for over two decades. Our unwavering commitment to excellence has established us as the premier choice for discerning homeowners.

We specialize in creating timeless, elegant kitchens and window treatments that perfectly balance aesthetics with functionality. Every project we undertake represents the pinnacle of craftsmanship and innovative design.

Our team works closely with you to turn your vision into reality, ensuring every detail exceeds expectations. From initial consultation to final installation, our process is tailored to deliver an experience as exceptional as the spaces we create.

With an extensive network of artisans and exclusive partnerships with leading European manufacturers, we offer access to materials and finishes unavailable elsewhere in the market.

Our Team

Giuseppe Cipriano

With over 25 years of experience in business development, project sales, and marketing within the contract construction industry, Giuseppe has built an exceptional track record.

He has successfully delivered more than 375 turnkey projects—ranging from $250K to $15M—for developers, interior designers, and construction managers.

Specializing in high-end kitchens, bathroom cabinetry, closets, and wardrobes, he brings deep technical expertise in design assist and bidding.

Most recently, Giuseppe has expanded into window treatments, completing 100 projects in just the first year.
